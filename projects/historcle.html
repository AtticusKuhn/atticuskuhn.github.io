Can you guess this historical figure?
By Atticus Kuhn
Published 5/1/2022
Tags: history, guessing, guessing game, wordle

I was inspired by the word-guessing game wordle to make my own spin on this fun idea. I am very passionate about history, so I decided to make a game where you guess a historical figure.

For each guess, you get to know the distance away they were born, how many years apart they were born and some commonalities. For example, if the secret person was Napoleon and you guessed, historicle would tell you they were born 9000 km apart, they were born 235 years apart, and both were commissioned military officers.

I think my game is really fun, so if you have any feedback, please contact me
